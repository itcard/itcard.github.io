How Do you Play This?

1) Split your deck of cards equally among players.

2) At the start of each round every player places a card of their choosing on the table.

3) Players take turns answering the opponents questions. If the answer is right, the player gets to steal the card. Whenever a card is taken, the owner of the stolen card must immediately place another on the table.

4) The turn of a player continues until he/she gets one wrong or doesn't know the answer to any of the questions on table. If someone goes on a stealing frenzy by getting multiple cards right, it’s up to other players to stop him by placing the meanest cards they’ve got..

5) Players get eliminated when they run out of cards. Last one standing wins.
